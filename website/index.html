How It Works

We look at your sales history, consider what's coming up (sales, holidays), and help you decide how much to order based on how much risk you're comfortable with.

Look at Past Sales

We analyze what you've sold recently to spot patterns. Are sales trending up? Down? Staying steady? This gives us a baseline for what to expect.

Factor in Events

Running a promotion? Holiday coming up? Offering a discount? These things change demand, so we adjust the forecast accordingly.

Show the Range

Forecasts aren't perfect. We show you a confidence range—best case, worst case, and most likely—so you know what you're working with.

Match Your Risk Style

Conservative? We'll recommend less to avoid overstock. Aggressive? We'll suggest more to capture every sale. You pick your comfort level.

Plain English Explanations

No jargon. Every recommendation comes with a clear explanation of why we're suggesting what we're suggesting.

No waiting around. Enter your data, hit submit, and get a recommendation in seconds.
